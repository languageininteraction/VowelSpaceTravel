About Vowel Space Travel

VowelSpaceTravel is a game that helps the player to learn sounds from a foreign language. The exercices adapt automatically to the level of the user, so that they are challenging while remaining doable. The player moves through a kind of galaxy where he can select planets in order to practice the vowels.

Language in Interaction Project

VowelSpaceTravel was developed within the research consortium Language in Interaction, which aims to explain variation and similarities in languages as well as in linguistic skills. The consortium aims to gain understanding of how such a large range of (cultural) differences have arisen from a biological basis that is common to all people.

The research program examines human language from the level of genetics to interactions between conversational partners, addressing a diverse range of topics such as: brain structure and function, language development, evolution, multilingualism, speech production and comprehension, nonverbal behavior, meaning and context, and the interplay of language with other cognitive systems (e.g. memory).

More language apps

The research results of Language in Interaction are translated into applications for society in areas such as education and health. Currently a range of language apps are developed, including games.
